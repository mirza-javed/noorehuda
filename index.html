Our Mission

At Noore Huda Online Quran Academy, our mission is to connect every Muslim child and teenager with the Holy Quran through qualified, caring and certified teachers — from anywhere in the world, at any time that suits your family. We believe every child deserves to recite the Quran beautifully, understand its meanings, and carry it in their heart. We are here to make that possible, insha'Allah.

Our Courses

دوراتنا
Beginner

Norani Qaida

نورانی قاعدہ

The perfect starting point for young learners. Students learn the Arabic alphabet, letter sounds, joining rules and basic pronunciation from scratch using the Noorani Qaida method — the most trusted foundation for Quran reading worldwide.

Intermediate

Nazira with Tajweed

ناظرہ مع تجوید

Learn to read the Holy Quran fluently with correct Tajweed rules — Makharij (articulation points), Sifaat (characteristics) and all essential pronunciation rules. Our certified teachers ensure every student recites with confidence and beauty.

Advanced

Hifz & Qiraat

حفظ و قراءات

A dedicated Hifz program for students committed to memorising the Holy Quran by heart. Combined with advanced Tajweed and beautiful Qiraat styles, this program is guided by experienced Huffaz teachers with a structured revision system.

Understanding

Fehm ul Quran

فہم القرآن

Go beyond recitation — understand what Allah says. This course teaches word-by-word Urdu and English translation, basic Tafseer and the practical lessons of Quranic verses for everyday life. Ideal for teenagers and adults.
