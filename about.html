Our Story

Noore Huda Online Quran Academy was founded with one simple belief: every Muslim child, wherever they live in the world, deserves access to qualified, authentic Quran education. We serve families in Pakistan, the United Kingdom, United States, Canada, the Gulf and beyond — bringing certified teachers directly into your home via live 1-on-1 online sessions. Our name, Noore Huda (نورِ ہدیٰ), means Light of Guidance — and that is exactly what we strive to bring to every child we teach.
